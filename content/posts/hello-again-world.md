I haven't maintained a blog for some time now, for no real reason at all except that I didn't have the will to write, or really to do much of anything. It's been a rough couple of years--not just COVID, but starting before that and amplified by COVID. I'm trying to dig my way out of that funk, and one of the ways I'm doing that is by starting to write again.

I have some things to write about--I'm learning Russian, and I have some side projects going here and there--and I might try to import some of the better posts from old blogs into this one. More than anything I want to get back into the habit of writing, as it's one of the things that has kept me centered in the past.

Hope all of you are having a good 2021.
